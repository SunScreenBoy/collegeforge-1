It's not all about GPA and SAT when you apply. I had classmates from my school with 3.4s and 1900s get into Berkeley for the same major I applied. Ignore stigmas you hear about colleges. Keep in mind that if you don't hear much about a school. Maybe that's because the people there are enjoying time themselves. Not necessarily broadcasting it, but they're still having a good time. Last thing is that a little self confidence goes a long way. Focus on something else in your essays and just be confident.
